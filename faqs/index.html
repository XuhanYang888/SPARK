General Questions

What is SPARK?
SPARK is a youth-led initiative that makes science fun and accessible for kids through hands-on activities, mentorship, and free or low-cost resources.
Who can join SPARK programs?
Our programs are designed for elementary and middle school students, but anyone interested in science is welcome to participate!
How much does it cost?
Most of our resources and activities are free or low-cost, thanks to our volunteers and supporters.
How can I volunteer?
High school and college students can volunteer as mentors or help organize events. Visit our Contact Us page to get started!
How do I start a SPARK branch?
We love expanding! Check out our "Start a Branch" info or reach out via the Contact page for guidance and resources.
