Start a Branch

By starting a SPARK branch at your school, you can bring exciting science experiences to younger students in your community.

Apply Now!

As a SPARK Branch, You Get:

Tools and Resources

Starter Package

Includes branding materials, outreach templates, event checklists, and planning guides to help you launch successfully.

Ready-to-Use Lesson Plans

Fully developed, age-appropriate lessons and hands-on science activities designed by experienced volunteers.

Monthly Resource Center Access

Ongoing updates with new lesson plans, outreach ideas, advertising templates, and experiments every month.

Sponsored Donations

Eligible branches may receive donated science kits, experiment materials, or funding to support their events.

Growth, Support & Recognition

Join the SPARK Community

Be part of a passionate, North America-wide network of student leaders and mentors.

Make Meaningful Connections

Collaborate with other branches, local schools, and organizations to broaden your impact.

Mentorship from Successful Branches

Learn from experienced leaders who will guide and support you along the way.

Leadership Skill Building

Gain valuable experience in team management, youth mentorship, public speaking, and program planning.

Official Recommendation Letter

Earn a letter of recommendation from SPARK's Executive Team to support academic, scholarship, or job applications.

Requirements

Enrolled in a NA High School

You're a current high school student in the U.S. or Canada, and your high school doesn't currently have a SPARK branch.

A Confident Leader

You can build and manage a small team, organize events, and take initiative to grow your branch.

Great with Kids

You enjoy working with younger students and can communicate ideas clearly, patiently, and enthusiastically.

Hands-On & Creative

You're ready to teach basic science concepts through engaging lessons and hands-on experiments.
