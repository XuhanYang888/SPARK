Our Mission

We offer our program for free, targeting underserved elementary schools to close the opportunity gap. Our high school volunteers understand the needs of the elementary school classrooms they were a part of only years ago.

Through hands-on experiments and creative exploration, we're showing students what science is really about: creativity and discovery.

Free Programs

All our educational programs are provided at no cost to participating schools.
