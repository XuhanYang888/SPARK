Science Projects and Research for Kids

SPARK was created to make science more exciting and accessible for younger students – especially those who may not have science clubs, STEM programs, or extra support at school.

Discover Our Programs

What We Do

Learn by Doing

We help younger kids learn science by doing, not just reading. Through experiments, projects, and creative challenges, we make science feel like play, while building real knowledge.

Close-mentorship

Our programs are led by high school students who understand how young minds learn. With near-peer mentorship, kids feel supported, understood, and inspired by role models who were just like them.

Accessibility

We design and distribute free or low-cost science kits, activity guides, and explainer videos, so every child—regardless of background—can explore and enjoy science at home or at school.

Why Spark?

Curiosity Ignites Discovery

Every scientist begins with a spark of curiosity about how the world works.

From Spark to Flame

We create programs that turn a spark of interest into a lifelong passion.
